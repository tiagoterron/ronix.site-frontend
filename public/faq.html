How to use Ronix with metamask.

Ronix is a experimental project, so the best way to keep your assets safe is creating a new account for using it.

In this tutorial I will show you how to use Ronix after creating a new account on MetaMask, receiving your assets (WETH, SLP, AXS, USDC) into this new account and then start using Ronix for transfering assets.

1. Download and install Metasmask:

Always make sure that you are visiting the right website: https://metamask.io

2. Create a new account:

Ronix does not require you to use your main account. So, let's create a new wallet where we will receive our assets from Ronin Wallet.

3. Connect your new account on Ronix:

Once you have created a new metamask account, connect your new account to http://ronix.site

4. Select the Ronin Network:

You will be able to connect to the RON Network after your account has been connected successfully

The Ronin Network (ChainId: 2020) is not connected to your metamask yet, so a confirmation will appear asking you if you want to add this network

Click Approve to proceed. Then Switch Network.

5. Claim some RON tokens:

By successfully connecting to the Ronin Network, you will be able to claim some RON tokens for transactions. Please click on CLAIM to receive 0.0005 RON tokens into this new account.

6. Transferring your assets from your Ronin Wallet to Metamask:

When our account is connected and we have some RON tokens for transactions, we need to obtain the assets (like WETH, AXS, SLP, etc) from our Ronin Wallet (main account) that we would like to swap or bridge to another network.

Copy the address of your new wallet, as shown below. In my case, the address is: 0x960b713d45b357eadb55d6e26e5b11614178e0c0

7. Go to your Ronin Wallet where your assets are located:

Access your ronin wallet

Select the account where you keep your assets on your Ronin wallet and send the assets to your new account. In my case I am sending to this address: 0x960b713d45b357eadb55d6e26e5b11614178e0c0

Due to the fact the Ronin wallet deals differently with wallet addresses, we need to change "0x..." to "ronin:..." like this: ronin:960b713d45b357eadb55d6e26e5b11614178e0c0

A confirmation screen will pop up

8. Go back to Ronix Website and check if you received your assets:

Your assets should appear in the Ronix session "My Wallet" if you followed those steps. Then you can already begin moving your assets between networks and trading them for other assets.

This is the safest way to keep your main account (where you keep your axies and your monthly earnings) safe.

This is a financial advise!
DO NOT USE YOUR MAIN ACCOUNT FOR SWAPPING TOKENS ON RONIX!
